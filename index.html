Who We Are

Rooted in Hawaiʻi,
built for the long arc.

For over two decades, Hawaiʻi Appleseed has brought together legal expertise, rigorous research, and community partnership to take on the root causes of poverty and inequality. We're a small team with a long view — and we believe the systems that shape everyday life can change.
