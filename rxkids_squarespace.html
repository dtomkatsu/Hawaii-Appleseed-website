What is RxKids?

RxKids is a groundbreaking program that provides no-strings-attached financial support to pregnant mothers. There are many successful RxKids programs in cities across Michigan.

💝

Universal Support

Every pregnant mother in participating communities receives support—no complicated eligibility requirements or means testing.

💵

No Strings Attached

Families decide how to use the funds based on their unique needs—whether for rent, food, baby supplies, or other essentials.

👶

Critical First Year

Monthly payments continue throughout a baby's first year, providing stability during the most important developmental period.

How RxKids would work in Hawaiʻi

A simple, dignified process that puts Hawaiian families first.

1

Pregnancy Enrollment

Pregnant mothers enroll in the program at 16+ weeks of pregnancy.

2

Initial Payment

Receive $1,500 during pregnancy to prepare for baby's arrival.

3

Monthly Support

After birth, receive $500 monthly throughout baby's first year.

Why Direct Cash Support Matters

Research shows that direct cash transfers to families improve maternal and infant health outcomes, reduce stress, and give children a stronger foundation for lifelong success. When families have financial security, everyone benefits.

Proven Benefits

Cash support programs like RxKids have demonstrated significant positive impacts on families and communities.

🏥

Better Health Outcomes

Reduced maternal stress and improved prenatal care lead to healthier pregnancies and births.

🧠

Child Development

Financial stability during early childhood supports cognitive and emotional development.

🏠

Housing Stability

Families can better afford rent and utilities, reducing housing insecurity.

🍎

Food Security

Parents can provide nutritious food for themselves and their growing families.

💼

Economic Mobility

Reduced financial stress allows parents to focus on work and career advancement.

❤️

Family Bonding

Less financial worry means more quality time for parents to bond with their newborns.
