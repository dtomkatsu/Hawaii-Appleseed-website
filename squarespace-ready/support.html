Policy Priorities

The issues we're
fighting for.

Hawaiʻi Appleseed focuses on the systems that shape everyday life — housing, wages, food, taxes, and transportation. These aren't abstract policy debates. They are the conditions that determine whether a family in Kalihi can afford to stay, whether a farmworker on Maui can see a doctor, whether a child in Waiʻanae has enough to eat.

View Full Policy Agenda
Affordable Housing

Tenant Protections & Housing Stability

Rising rents and displacement are crisis-level issues across our islands. We're pushing for stronger tenant protections and more deeply affordable housing so that people can afford to stay in the communities they call home.

Food Equity

Expanding Food Access for All Families

No family in Hawaiʻi should go hungry. We advocate for policies that strengthen SNAP, expand school meal programs, and support access to culturally significant, nutritious food in every community.

Wages & Labor

Fair Wages for Hawaiʻi's Workers

A living wage isn't radical — it's a baseline. We support legislation that ensures every worker across our islands can afford to live here, from farmworkers to service employees to care workers.

Transportation Equity

Safer Streets & Affordable Transit

Getting where you need to go shouldn't be a barrier to opportunity. We advocate for safe, accessible, and affordable transportation so every resident can reach jobs, school, and care — regardless of whether they drive.

Taxes & Budget

A Fairer Tax Code for a Thriving Hawaiʻi

Hawaiʻi's tax system asks too much of those who have the least. We push for tax relief for low- and middle-income families and for the wealthy to pay their fair share — so we can fund the public investments our communities need.
