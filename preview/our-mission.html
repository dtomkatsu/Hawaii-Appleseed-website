Our Mission

Advancing economic justice for and with the people of Hawaiʻi.

A more socially and economically just Hawaiʻi, where everyone has genuine opportunities to achieve economic security and fulfill their potential.

Five core values

Collaboration

We work with diverse partners to build durable connections.

People First

Change must center those most adversely impacted by current systems.

Equity

We name structural inequities — racism, colonialism, oppression.

Courage

We speak up against powerful interests for people-first policies.

Integrity

Our work is accurate, reliable, and aligned with our stated values.

A Guiding Principle

Those closest to the problem are closest to the solution.

We integrate people with lived experience of these issues into our leadership and decision-making.
